About Me

The thought of chiseling away at problems for hours or days on end and then seeing the results of your labor is something that drives me. Full Stack Development allows me to be creative, innovative and analytical at the same time and these are I that I have ever wanted to find in a career.

When I'm not coding you can find me meditating and contemplating the vastness of the universe.
