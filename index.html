About Me

I was born in Chicago, IL on Dec. 17, 1989. As a child I always loved building models and completing puzzles. Figuring out how everything should be put together and seeing the completed result always gave me a sense of joy. I hope to apply this same passion to building websites, apps, and games!


The reason I have decided to pursue a career in coding is because of the ever changing nature of the field. There is always something new to learn!


The community that is involved in coding is also something that attracted me. Most things are open source and anyone can contribute.
